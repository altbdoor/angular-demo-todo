Just a demonstration of sorts with a to-do list, to try out Angular JS.

It is a very, very basic to-do list, with only simple title and description. All data is stored with localStorage, which will take up space on your browser. No information whatsover is sent to anywhere.

Developed with:

  • Bootstrap 3.3.5
  • AngularJS 1.3.15
  • lscache
